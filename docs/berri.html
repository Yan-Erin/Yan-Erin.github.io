Berri.io

Frontend devloper/UI
During the COVID-19 pandemic, many students found it harder to meet people and become friends due to social distancing. Berri.io is a website and app that helps students from 11 different colleges meet each other anonymously through cute fruit avatars! Just fill out your name, classes, and interests and you are good to go!
In the Berri team, I helped design and create the about me and patch notes pages as well some styling on the app.
