About Me

Woah! A whole page about me?

Hey, I'm Erin, a freshman pursuing a BS in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University. I'm a chaotic-good learner, an aspiring gadget creator, and most importantly a curious discoverer 🗺️.

I'm currently working as a frontend developer at Berri.io, a website that helps college students cultivate fruity friendships 🍋 anonymously! Previously I interned at Google CSSI and used JavaScript to create a Bomberman game. I also interned at MIT Beaverworks, creating a 3D printer and a filament recycling machine.
